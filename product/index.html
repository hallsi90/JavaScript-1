Our most versatile waterproof jacket.

Rain Slayer is designed to protect you from the most severe weather conditions. It is made with a premium Gore-Tex fabric which is the world-leading membrane for durable waterproofness (28.000mm), optimized breathability (RET 9), and total windproofness. Therefore Rain Slayer have an impressive ability to repel water, keep you dry, and protect you from harsh wind gusts.

The jacket comes equipped with special features, such as sealed seams and waterproof zippers, which provide an additional barrier against rain and wind. It also have adjustable hood, cuffs and waistband to ensure a comfortable, snug fit that keeps the elements out.

The Rain Slayer jacket are available in various sizes and colours to fit everyones needs and wants.

This versital jacket is suitable for various outdoor activities, such as hiking, skiing, climbing and camping. With a Rain Slayer jacket, you can confidently brave stormy weather conditions and stay dry and comfortable.

Key features:

  • Waterproof and breathable Gore-Tex fabric.
  • Sealed seams and zippers to prevent water from leaking through stitching and seams.
  • Adjustable hood with a brim and drawstring to keep rain off the head and face.
  • Velcro adjustable cuffs and elasticized waist to ensure a snug, comfortable fit that stays in place during movement.
  • Zippered pockets for conveniently storing belongings and keeping them dry.
  • Long underarm vents with two-way zipper to ensure good ventilation in warm weather.
  • Reflectinve detailing for visibility in low-light conditions.
  • Stowable hood for versatility depending on weather conditions.
  • Available in different colours to suit personal style preferences.
  • Durable and lightweight for a variety of outdoor activities.
